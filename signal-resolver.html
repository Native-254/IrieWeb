Signal Resolver

The resolver merges multiple strategy opinions into one action while checking current position state so the bot does not double-enter or short what it cannot short.

Multiple strategies can disagree. One may want to buy, another may want to sell, and a third may prefer no action. The resolver converts that noisy input into a single safe instruction.

  • Consensus and priority: signal votes can be merged through majority, weighted priority, or explicit strategy ranking.
  • Position awareness: the resolver checks whether the symbol is flat, long, or short before deciding.
  • Safe reversal: when changing direction, it closes the existing position before opening the opposite side.
  • Naked short prevention: it rejects short actions when the account, broker mode, or current holding state cannot support them.
