Backtesting

The backtesting engine validates strategy behavior on historical data and reports the metrics needed before live trading is considered.

back to card

backtest reportHISTORICAL

Backtesting does not prove the future, but it does expose broken assumptions, unprofitable rules, excessive drawdown, and execution-sensitive strategies before they touch a broker.

MetricWhy it matters
Total returnShows raw strategy growth over the test period.
Sharpe ratioCompares return to volatility, helping identify smoother strategies.
Max drawdownMeasures the deepest equity decline and informs risk limits.
Win rateUseful only with average win/loss size, but helpful for strategy character.
Trade countReveals whether results come from enough samples to take seriously.
