The bot is arranged as independent services connected by clear contracts, so each layer can be swapped, tested, or extended without rewriting the whole system.

open architecture page

Strategies calculate their own indicators and return normalized signals, allowing the resolver to decide how the portfolio should act.

open strategies page

The resolver prevents duplicate entries, closes before reversing, and avoids naked shorting by respecting current holdings before issuing orders.

open resolver page

Risk is treated as the operating system: every trade must pass position sizing, exposure, loss-limit, and stop-placement checks.

open risk page

The pipeline fetches market bars, caches reusable data, protects provider limits, and keeps the rest of the bot independent from source-specific quirks.

open data page

The execution layer translates approved portfolio intent into broker-ready orders while preserving a paper-mode safety lane.

open execution page

Alerts keep the operator informed when trades fire, safeguards trip, connectivity changes, or background services need attention.

open alerts page

The backtesting engine measures strategy behavior before live deployment, highlighting returns, drawdown, win rate, Sharpe, and trade quality.

open backtesting page

The dashboard exposes operational endpoints and human-readable views for monitoring the bot without tailing logs all day.

open dashboard page

Deployment turns the bot into a managed service with predictable startup, logs, environment files, firewall rules, and recovery behavior.
