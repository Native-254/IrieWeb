01 / THE MACHINE

      
Built from scratch. Runs like a headless VPS ghost.

      
No black-box SaaS, no drag-and-drop bot builder. Every module — data pipeline, signal resolver, broker layer, risk manager — is hand-written Python, wired together with dependency injection so any piece can be swapped or unit-tested on its own.

      
    
        
  • Signal resolver — merges every strategy's output and only ever proposes actions consistent with what you actually hold. No accidental naked shorts.
    • 
        
  • Broker abstraction — Interactive Brokers via ib_async today, with Binance, OKX and more behind the same interface.
    • 
        
  • Realistic paper simulation — slippage, commissions, partial fills and short-availability checks, so the paper account behaves like a live one.
    • 
        
  • Position sync — internal state reconciled against IBKR's reported positions on every single iteration.
    •

02 / HOW IT THINKS

    
Two strategies. One resolver. Zero double-guessing.

    
Every strategy votes. The resolver only ever acts on votes that make sense given what the book is already holding — a Breakout module (Donchian channels) is next on the roadmap.


    

      

        
strategies/trend_following_ls.py

        
Trend-Following L/S

        
Long when the 50-SMA clears the 200-SMA and RSI dips under 30. Short the mirror image — 50 below 200, RSI over 70. Bracket orders on both sides, no naked exposure.

        

          IF sma50 > sma200 AND rsi < 30 → ENTER_LONG
          IF sma50 < sma200 AND rsi > 70 → ENTER_SHORT
        

      

      

        
strategies/mean_reversion.py

        
Mean-Reversion

        
Bollinger Bands paired with RSI. Buys the fear when price snaps below the lower band with RSI under 30, exits the euphoria above the upper band with RSI over 70.

        

          IF price < lower_bb AND rsi < 30 → ENTER_LONG
          IF price > upper_bb AND rsi > 70 → EXIT_LONG
        

      

    


    
Backtested with vectorbt. New strategies must clear the same 5-year backtest before they ever touch paper capital — see Rulebook §5.

  







  

  

    
03 / BUILT-IN RESTRAINT

    
The bot is only as smart as the limits it can't override.

    
Every rule below runs automatically, before a single order reaches Interactive Brokers. None of it is a suggestion.


    

      

        

        
Checked before it's sent

        
Short-availability checks confirm shares actually exist to borrow. Position sync reconciles internal state against IBKR every iteration. An earnings blackout filter blocks new entries near a print.

      

      

        

        
Protected on the way down

        
ATR-based stops on every position. Trailing stops tighten as a trade works. Drawdown past 15% halves size automatically; past 20% halts trading outright for a re-evaluation.

      

      

        

        
Sized like it's real money

        
Half-Kelly position sizing off recent win/loss history, capped at 5% of equity per trade. Portfolio heat capped at 30%. Gross exposure capped at a safe multiple of equity. Daily loss limit: -5%.

04 / THE RULEBOOK

      
Governance, not vibes.

      
Seven standing rules keep the strategy honest before it ever sees live capital. They're enforced on a calendar, not on a mood.

      
    
        
  1. Minimum sample size. No edge is real before 50 closed trades and 12 months of live-like paper data.
    2. 
        
  2. Max drawdown. 15% drawdown halves size. 20% halts trading for re-evaluation.
    3. 
        
  3. Daily loss limit. Trading stops for the day at -5% equity, enforced by the risk manager itself.
    4. 
        
  4. Review cadence. Weekly Sharpe & exposure review, monthly robustness backtests, quarterly paper-vs-backtest audit.
    5. 
        
  5. Strategy changes. Any parameter change needs a 5-year backtest before it's allowed near paper capital.
    6. 
        
  6. Emotional discipline. No manual overrides. No sizing up on euphoria, no sizing down on fear.
    7. 
        
  7. Go-live criteria. 12 months live-like, Sharpe > 0.5, max drawdown < 15%, win rate within one σ of backtest.

05 / SIGNAL RECEIVED

      
It doesn't trade quietly.

      
Every fill, every error, every risk-limit trip gets pushed out in real time — across three independent channels, so a dead webhook never means a blind spot.

      

        

          
01

          
Discord

          
Rich embeds — symbol, action, quantity, price — plus dedicated error alerts, delivered via webhook.

        

        

          
02

          
Telegram

          
Plain-text trade alerts through a bot token and chat ID, for the moments Discord isn't in reach.

        

        

          
03

          
Email

          
Brevo API as primary transport, Gmail SMTP as fallback — critical errors always find an inbox.

        

      

    

    

      

        
      

    

  







  

  

    

      
    

    

      
READ BEFORE YOU TRADE

      
This is an educational project. Treat it like one.

      
IrieTrade is for learning, not financial advice. Past performance never guarantees future results, paper P&L isn't live P&L, and the rulebook exists precisely because the strategy hasn't earned real capital yet — see Rule 1 and Rule 7. Test thoroughly. Trade at your own risk.
